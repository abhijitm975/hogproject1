Expectations

Reality
Academic Freedom: Many students expect to have more freedom in their coursework, such as choosing their own topics for essays and projects. Academic Freedom: In reality, students often find that they have limited freedom in their coursework and that they must conform to the expectations and guidelines set by their professors.
Social Life: Students expect to make a lot of friends and have an active social life, filled with parties and other events. Social Life: While students can certainly make friends in college, they may find that they don't have as much free time as they expected and that they must balance their social life with their academic responsibilities.
Preparedness for Career: Students expect that their college education will fully prepare them for their desired career path. Preparedness for Career: College provides a strong foundation for a future career, but students may find that they still need additional training and experience before they are fully prepared for their chosen field.
